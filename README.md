job description
Video game designers specify innovative game and role-play mechanics, story lines, and character biographies. They create and maintain design documentation, and guide and collaborate with production staff to produce games as designed. They devise missions, challenges, or puzzles to be encountered in game play, balance and adjust gameplay experiences to ensure the critical and commercial success of the product, conduct regular design reviews throughout the game development process, and oversee gameplay testing to ensure intended gaming experience and adherence to the original vision. Video game designers must also keep abreast of game design technology and techniques, industry trends, or audience interests, reactions, and needs by reviewing current literature, talking with colleagues, participating in educational programs, attending meetings or workshops, or participating in professional organizations or conferences.
